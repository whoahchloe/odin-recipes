Banana Bread

Description

This banana bread recipe creates the most delicious, moist loaf with loads of banana flavour. Why compromise the banana flavor? Friends and family love my recipe and say it's by far the best! It tastes wonderful toasted.

Our most popular banana bread is moist, delicious, and absolutely packed with banana flavour. This community favorite will quickly become your go-to banana bread recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 ⅓ cups mashed overripe bananas
